Innovative Approach to Oyster Farming

🇸🇮 Slovenščina

About the project

This project aims to restore the European flat oyster (Ostrea edulis) in the Slovenian sea using an ecological mariculture model.

Main activities

  • Establishing farming structures
  • Using innovative and eco-friendly materials
  • Collaboration with research institutions
  • Promotion of sustainable aquaculture

Objectives and results

  • Restocking of Ostrea edulis species
  • Establishment of a working oyster farming model
  • Enhancing the economic value of marine products
